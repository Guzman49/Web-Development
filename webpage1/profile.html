Joaquín “El Chapo” Guzmán

The rise and fall of one of the world's most notorious cartel leaders

Joaquín "El Chapo" Guzmán was born on April 4, 1957, in Badiraguato, Sinaloa, Mexico. Raised in a rural farming family, he later became one of the most powerful figures in international drug trafficking.

Guzmán expanded his influence through the Sinaloa Cartel, building extensive international trafficking routes and becoming one of the most wanted individuals by global law enforcement agencies.

Guzmán's activities had a significant impact on global drug trafficking and organized crime enforcement efforts. His story remains one of the most studied cases in modern criminal history.
